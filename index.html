Dear Navya ❤️

If you're reading this, it means you made it through every episode — the clumsy pilot, the slow-burn middle, the messy bits we didn't plan for — and you're still here, still choosing this story. I don't think I'll ever find the right words for what that means to me, but I'm going to try anyway.

Thank you for eighteen months of feeling like the main character in my own life for the first time. Thank you for the laughter that sneaks up on me on ordinary Tuesdays, for the way you make a quiet night in feel like an event, and for loving me on the days I didn't make it easy to. You turned an ordinary timeline into something worth marking, worth celebrating, worth building a whole website about at 1am.

I'm grateful for the version of me that exists because of you — calmer, softer, more sure of what matters. And I'm so excited for everything we haven't filmed yet: the trips we'll take, the home we'll build, the inside jokes we haven't even thought of. I want every season after this one, too.

Thank you for being my favorite story.
Happy 1.5 Years ❤️
Forever my favorite series.

— Yours, always
